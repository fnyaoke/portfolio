About Me

Florence holds a degree in BSc. General (Physics Major) from the University of Nairobi and a diploma in management of information systems (IMIS) from Strathmore University She has worked in several spheres including the entertainment industry, human rights advocacy and humanitarian works.

She is a social justice advocate championing for equality and the rights of all persons. Believeing that all opression is interconnected and that to stop oppression persons have to work together, she practices the Ubuntu concept that says 'I am because you are' Her spaces of influence include:

  1. Peace Justice and strong institutions (SDG 16) - enhancing rehabilitation and promoting successful and sustainable rehabilitation of ex-offenderd
  2. Good health and wellbeing (SDG 3) - Promoting access to holistic healthcare for persons living with sickle cell disease
  3. Quality Education (SDG 4) - Creating continuous life long learning opportunities for the youth and volunteers
  4. Reduced Inequalities (SDG 10) - Advocating for the rights of all oppressed with focus on LGBTIQ Rights
